How to Submit Your Business to Google Maps

Whether you want to submit your business to Google yourself or have us do it for you, here's everything you need to know about getting listed on Google Maps in Nigeria.

Step-by-Step: Submit Your Business to Google

1

Go to Google Business Profile

Visit business.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click "Add your business to Google" to begin the submission process.

2

Enter Your Business Name & Category

Type your business name exactly as registered. Select the most relevant category (e.g. "Restaurant", "Auto Repair Shop"). This is critical for appearing in the right local searches.

3

Add Location & Service Areas

Pin your business location on the map. If you serve multiple areas in Nigeria, add them as service areas. Accurate location data is key to ranking in "near me" searches.

4

Verify Your Business with Google

Google will verify your business via postcard, phone call, or video. This step confirms you are the legitimate owner and activates your listing on Google Maps.

5

Optimize & Go Live

Add your phone number, opening hours, photos, and a keyword-rich description. Then submit — your local business is now live on Google Maps and Google Search.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a Google review for a business? +

Search for the business on Google or Google Maps, click on the listing, scroll to the "Reviews" section, and click "Write a review". Rate 1–5 stars and add your comments. You must be signed into a Google account. Positive reviews help businesses rank higher in the Nigerian business directory results.

How do I submit a Google business review as a customer? +

Open Google Maps on your phone or browser, find the business you visited, tap "Reviews", then tap the star rating you want to give. You can write a short description of your experience and optionally add photos. Your review will appear publicly on their Google listing.
